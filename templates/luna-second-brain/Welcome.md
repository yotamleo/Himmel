# Welcome

This vault was instantiated from the
[luna-brain](https://github.com/yotamleo/Himmel/tree/main/templates/luna-second-brain) skeleton. The
skeleton ships a PARA folder layout, daily/weekly templates, and a
Claude operating manual.

**First read:**

- [[_CLAUDE.md]] — how Claude operates in this vault (folder map,
  auto-save rules, AI-first vault rule).
- [[index]] — catalog of all notes.
- [[log]] — chronological log of vault changes.

**Make this vault your own:**

1. Edit `_CLAUDE.md` to fill in the `{{OWNER_NAME}}`, `{{VAULT_NAME}}`,
   `{{VAULT_PURPOSE}}`, and `{{CREATED_DATE}}` placeholders.
2. Replace this Welcome page with your own dashboard, or delete it.
3. Start capturing into `00-Inbox/`.

Setup details live in [`README.md`](README.md).
